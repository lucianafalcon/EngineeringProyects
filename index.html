    • Notch LP Filter
The designed Notch Twin-T filter is a band-reject filter in cascade with a low-pass filter. Its main function is to eliminate the specific frequency of 79.5 Hz while allowing the rest of the signal to pass and attenuating frequencies above 954.92 Hz due to the low-pass response. This type of filter is useful in cardiac monitoring devices, such as electrocardiographs (ECG), where power line interference or muscle noise at low frequencies is common. By adjusting the notch values, it is possible to clean the signal and improve monitoring accuracy.
